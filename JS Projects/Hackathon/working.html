How It Works

Understand how you can report road issues in just a few steps.

📋 Step-by-Step Guide

📸 Step 1: Capture Photo

Take a clear photo of the pothole or waterlogged road area using your phone.

📍 Step 2: Auto Geolocation

We automatically detect your GPS location to tag the issue precisely on the map.

📝 Step 3: Add Details

Write a short description of the issue – how big it is or if it's dangerous.

🗺️ Step 4: View on Map

After submission, your report appears on our live map for everyone to see.

✅ Step 5: Action

Local authorities are notified and can prioritize urgent fixes via dashboard.

💡 Why Use FixMyRoad?

  • ⚡ Quick and easy way to report problems
  • 📡 Real-time tracking of road issues
  • 👥 Community-powered platform
  • 🔒 No personal data collected
